# Student Attendance Management System
The objective of the Student Management System is to automate the process of attendance calculation manually.

## Objectives:

- Attendance entry each day.
- Generate Monthly Attendance and show percentage calculation below 75%.
- Cumulative Attendance taken each month allows Student and University to
  view Attendance Records.
- More than 30 classes are considered as 30.
- Show Eligible / Not Eligible
- Store Attendance Data in a Centralized Database.
